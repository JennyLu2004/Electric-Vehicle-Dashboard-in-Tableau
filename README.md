# Electric Vehicle Population & Market Analysis Dashboard

An interactive Tableau dashboard designed to analyze the growth, adoption trends, geographical distribution, and technological breakdown of Electric Vehicles (EVs) using real-world vehicle registration data.

**[View Live Interactive Dashboard on Tableau Public](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/thao.lu6343/viz/Electric_Vehicle_Dashboard_17324843935220/Dashboard1)**

---

## Project Overview

As electric vehicles continue to redefine the automotive industry, understanding adoption patterns, range efficiency, and market share is critical for urban planners, manufacturers, and policy analysts.

This project transforms raw vehicle registration data into a dynamic business intelligence tool. The dashboard enables users to monitor key metrics, evaluate Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) vs. Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs), track adoption growth over model years, and identify leading manufacturers in the market.

---

## Key Features & Metrics

### 1. Executive Summary KPIs
* **Total Vehicle Volume:** Comprehensive count of registered electric vehicles in the dataset.
* **Average Electric Range:** High-level metric tracking battery capacity and mileage capabilities across models.
* **Vehicle Technology Breakdown:** Total count and percentage distribution of **BEVs** (fully electric) vs. **PHEVs** (plug-in hybrid).

### 2. Market Trends & Analytics
* **Adoption Over Time (Model Year):** Visualizes growth trajectories and historical adoption surges across model years.
* **Top Manufacturers (Make Analysis):** Ranks the top EV brands to highlight market dominance and competitive landscape.
* **Geographic Distribution:** Maps vehicle concentration by state/county to identify regional adoption hubs.
* **CAFV Eligibility Breakdown:** Categorizes vehicles based on Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle (CAFV) eligibility status.

### 3. Interactive Filtering & Drill-Downs
* Global dynamic filters allowing stakeholders to slice data by **Model Year**, **Vehicle Type (BEV/PHEV)**, and **Make/Model** for granular exploratory analysis.

## Technical Skills Demonstrated

* **Data Visualization & Storytelling:** Structured KPI cards, clean layout hierarchy, and intentional color palette for executive readability.
* **Calculated Fields & Expressions:** Used LOD (Level of Detail) expressions, aggregations, and ratio calculations to determine fleet percentages and averages dynamically.
* **Interactive Dashboard Design:** Configured parameter actions, field filters, dynamic tooltips, and cross-filtering across views.
* **Business Intelligence Best Practices:** Balanced macro trends (market adoption) with micro insights (brand-level specs) to drive actionable exploration.
