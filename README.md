# ⚡ Electric Vehicle Market & Adoption Dashboard

An interactive Tableau dashboard exploring electric vehicle (EV) registration trends, battery tech adoption, and market leaders. Built to make complex automotive data clean, visual, and easy to navigate.

👉 **[Check out the live interactive dashboard on Tableau Public](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/thao.lu6343/viz/Electric_Vehicle_Dashboard_17324843935220/Dashboard1)**

---

## What This Dashboard Does

With EV adoption growing fast, I wanted to build a business intelligence tool that answers three main questions: **How fast is the market growing, what tech is leading, and who dominates the space?**

Here's what you can explore in the dashboard:

* **Top-Line KPIs:** Quickly see total EV count, average electric range, and the overall market split between **BEVs** (fully electric) and **PHEVs** (plug-in hybrids).
* **Adoption Over Time:** Track how EV registrations have trended across model years.
* **Brand & Tech Breakdown:** See which manufacturers are leading the market and how CAFV (Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle) eligibility breaks down.
* **Geographic Trends:** Map where EV concentrations are highest by state and county.
* **Interactive Filters:** Slice and filter the data by Model Year, Make, or Vehicle Type to spot specific trends.

## Technical Highlights

* **Tableau & BI Design:** Designed a clear layout hierarchy with dynamic tooltips and global cross-filtering for seamless exploration.
* **Calculations & Logic:** Used custom calculated fields and aggregations to compute live averages, ratios, and percentages.
* **Data Storytelling:** Focused on balanced visual design so both non-technical users and analysts can pull quick insights.
