# Electric Vehicle Market & Adoption Analytics Dashboard

This is an interactive dashboard built in Tableau to analyze electric vehicle (EV) registration trends, battery technology, and manufacturer market share across key geographic regions.

I developed this project to sharpen my hands-on skills in data visualization and designing a clean and interactive dashboard that turns data into practical business insights.

[View Interactive Dashboard on Tableau Public](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/thao.lu6343/viz/Electric_Vehicle_Dashboard_17324843935220/Dashboard1)

---

## What This Dashboard Does

This tool turns raw vehicle registration data into visual insights to help urban planners, energy providers, and analysts answer key strategic questions:

* **Infrastructure & Charging Locations:** Where should energy companies and cities build new charging stations?  
  Maps high-density EV areas and identifies regions with shorter average battery ranges that need fast-charging options.
* **Grid Planning & Power Demand:** How will new EV registrations affect local utility grids?  
  Tracks the ratio of fully electric vehicles (BEVs) versus plug-in hybrids (PHEVs) to help utility companies plan for higher power demand.
* **Market Share & Brand Performance:** Which car brands are leading the market, and how are competitors catching up?  
  Ranks top manufacturers by registration count and tracks market share shifts over model years.

---

## Core Dashboard Features

* **Summary KPI Cards:** Quick overview of total EV volume, average electric range, and BEV vs. PHEV market split.
* **Geographic Mapping:** Location analysis by state and county to spot high-growth regions.
* **Trend Analysis:** Registration patterns tracked across model years to measure market growth over time.
* **Interactive Filters:** Dropdown controls for **Model Year**, **Vehicle Type**, and **Make/Model** for deeper data exploration.
